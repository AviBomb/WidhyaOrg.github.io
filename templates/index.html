About Us

How Will Widhya Be With Ya?

Hi I'm Widhya! Widhya is a platform that aims to solve the problem of the near failure of the education system. Focuses on a "Learning By Doing" approach, thereby removing the concept of rote learning. Widhya doesn't judge you based on your marks and grades, but all we that matters is, your quality of work.

The best part is that anybody pursuing any course can choose to learn about and work on any concept on the platform. Learning, in my language, does not mean going through a set of lectures or going to class and listening to your professors speak. Learning, in my language means, to learn while doing and how will that happen you might wonder!

I offer micro-tasks that you could solve and on solving a set of micro-tasks

What are Micro-Tasks?

Micro-Tasks are real time industrial projects given to me by various companies. These micro-tasks are small scale problems that you will have to solve in order to complete a Mission. A Mission is a set of micro-tasks and every mission will have some material that you can refer to, incase you get stuck.

On successfully completing a Mission, I will personally recommend you for an internship at the partner company from whom I got this Mission. Either way, I'll make sure you get a certificate of achievement from my side and the parent company's side!

Sounds great right!?
