What is Widhya?

The kingdom of knowledge has kicked off a path of an ideological renaissance, with numerous teaching and learning techniques. We believe in creating a symbiosis of education and technology which brings up the augment ordinary teaching and learning skills to new pinnacles.

We provide a platform for young talents to showcase their skills, network with the and create opportunities with an identity to be recognized by the well established personalities of industries and to create a portal for an epitome of diverse skills. It’s no secret that internships are the most effective way for students to gain exposure before graduation and kick start their careers.

At Widhya, we aim towards building a unique platform bringing students and employers together under one roof, helping employers find grassroots of their companies by choosing students. Our core focus is to remove the concept of rote learning, enabling students to focus on learning by doing and help recruiters find the real cream from the pool of talent, trying to solve the near failure of India's education system.

Our Core Values

We are inquisitive and curious about everything. Learning is the most important part of our process. We prioritize long term expertise over short term gains.

Innovate Fearlessly

We are not afraid of failure. We embrace challenge. We support and empower each other to go against the status quo.

Be Data Driven

When it can be measured- we measure it. KPIs > vanity metrics. We are always thinking about what data we have and how to use it.

Default to Transparency

We value honest and open communication. We communicate relationships, processes and what is going on "behind the scenes".
We believe sharing information is the best way to foster transparency in our field.

Take Action

We focus on what we can accomplish today. Small wins lead to big success. We take ownership. We see projects through and communicate results.

We put our communities’ goals first. We talk in terms of their priorities, helping them to discover what they want and how to achieve it. We seek feedback from our
community constantly. We engage in conversations, not monologues.

Give Warm Fuzzies

We support the team. We give both positive and constructive feedback regularly. We share information proactively. We are collaborative and ego-less when
searching for the best ideas.

Care Intensely

We work passionately (but not obsessively… usually). We constantly pursue projects that excite us and align with our mission.
